# Digital Marketing Proposal: Lueur Skin Clinic

**Prepared for:** Lueur (formerly Ozmedica)  
**Location:** Melbourne, Australia  
**Budget:** $7,500 AUD / month  
**Date:** March 2026  

---

## 1. Executive Summary

The Melbourne skincare market is highly saturated and competitive, with clinics clustered heavily around inner-city hubs like South Yarra and Chapel Street. Standing out against established brands such as Aesop, Sukin, Go-To Skin Care, and dozens of premium local clinics requires a comprehensive, multi-channel approach. 

This proposal outlines a realistic, performance-driven digital marketing strategy tailored specifically for Lueur. With a monthly budget of $7,500 AUD, our focus will be on sustainable growth across SEO, Google Ads, Social Media, and a complete Website Redesign. We understand that Lueur is a service-based business offering premium skin treatments and consultations, not an ecommerce store. Therefore, our primary metric for success is high-quality lead generation and booking conversions.

We do not believe in overnight miracles. In a competitive landscape like Melbourne, building authority and achieving sustainable ROI takes time. This proposal is built on honesty, setting realistic benchmarks rather than making unachievable guarantees.

---

## 2. Market Context & Challenges

Operating a skin clinic in Melbourne presents unique challenges that require strategic navigation:

- **High Competition:** The inner suburbs of Melbourne are densely populated with premium aesthetic and skin clinics. Brand positioning must be distinct to capture market share.
- **Rising Acquisition Costs:** The cost of digital advertising in the health and beauty sector has steadily increased. Average Cost Per Click (CPC) on Google Ads for high-intent service terms (e.g., "skin clinic Melbourne") ranges from $15 to $25+, while branded or product terms sit between $6 and $12. New accounts without historical quality scores may experience even higher initial costs.
- **Trust and Authority:** Consumers seeking skin treatments require a high level of trust before booking. Educational content, before-and-after imagery, and transparent pricing are critical for conversion.

---

## 3. Strategic Approach & Deliverables

Our approach allocates the $7,500 AUD monthly budget across six core pillars to ensure a balanced and effective marketing ecosystem.

### 3.1 Website Redesign & Optimization
The foundation of any digital strategy is a high-converting website. We will oversee a complete redesign of the Lueur website to ensure it reflects the premium nature of your services.
- **Focus:** User experience (UX), mobile responsiveness, and clear conversion pathways for booking consultations.
- **Content:** Development of dedicated, single-issue landing pages for specific treatments (e.g., acne, pigmentation, anti-aging) which have been proven to increase conversion rates in the cosmetic sector.

### 3.2 Search Engine Optimization (SEO)
SEO is a long-term investment essential for reducing reliance on paid advertising over time.
- **Local SEO:** Optimization of your Google Business Profile to capture "near me" searches in Melbourne and surrounding suburbs.
- **Content Strategy:** Creating authoritative content addressing common skin concerns to build trust and capture top-of-funnel traffic.
- **Technical SEO:** Ensuring the new website is fast, secure, and easily crawlable by search engines.

### 3.3 Google Ads (Search & Local)
Google Ads will drive immediate, high-intent traffic to the clinic while the SEO strategy matures.
- **Targeting:** Focusing on specific, high-margin treatments rather than broad, highly competitive terms to maximize budget efficiency.
- **Budget Management:** Careful monitoring of CPCs, acknowledging the $15-$25+ reality for competitive terms, and optimizing for Cost Per Lead (CPL).
- **Ad Copy & Extensions:** Highlighting unique selling propositions, clear pricing guidance, and easy booking options.

### 3.4 Paid Social Media Advertising (Meta Ads)
Social ads are highly effective for visual services like skin treatments.
- **Campaign Types:** Retargeting website visitors and reaching new local audiences through visually compelling ads.
- **Creative:** Utilizing authentic before-and-after imagery (adhering to medical advertising guidelines) and educational video content.
- **Cost Efficiency:** Leveraging lower CPCs on Meta platforms (typically $1.20 - $2.80) to drive brand awareness and initial inquiries.

### 3.5 Organic Social Media Management
Building a community and showcasing expertise through organic channels.
- **Platforms:** Instagram and TikTok, focusing on visual transformations, behind-the-scenes clinic footage, and practitioner expertise.
- **Content Pillars:** Education, social proof (testimonials/results), and treatment spotlights.

### 3.6 Analytics & Reporting
Transparent tracking of all activities to measure performance against benchmarks.
- **Setup:** Implementation of Google Analytics 4 (GA4) and conversion tracking for all booking forms and calls.
- **Reporting:** Comprehensive monthly reports detailing traffic, CPL, booking numbers, and strategic insights.

## 4. Realistic Timeline & Milestones

We frame our targets as benchmarks we work toward, not guaranteed promises. The following timeline outlines realistic expectations for a new campaign in the Melbourne market.

### Months 1-3: Foundation & Testing
- **Focus:** Launching the new website, setting up tracking, and initiating paid campaigns.
- **Expectations:** CPCs and CPLs will be at their highest as the ad accounts build history and quality scores. SEO will show minimal impact. The primary goal is data collection, identifying which treatments and messaging resonate best with the Melbourne audience.
- **Milestone:** Stable lead flow established; baseline metrics (CPC, CVR, CPL) defined.

### Months 4-6: Optimization & Early Growth
- **Focus:** Refining ad copy, adjusting bids based on performance data, and accelerating content creation for SEO.
- **Expectations:** We anticipate a gradual decrease in CPL as campaigns become more efficient. Initial movements in local SEO rankings should begin to drive organic traffic.
- **Milestone:** 15-20% improvement in ad conversion rates; measurable increase in organic website traffic.

### Months 7-12: Scaling & Authority Building
- **Focus:** Scaling successful campaigns, expanding SEO reach, and maximizing return on ad spend (ROAS).
- **Expectations:** Paid campaigns will operate at peak efficiency. SEO efforts will begin to yield consistent, high-quality organic leads, reducing the overall blended cost of acquisition.
- **Milestone:** Established presence for key treatment terms in the local Melbourne market; consistent month-over-month booking growth.
