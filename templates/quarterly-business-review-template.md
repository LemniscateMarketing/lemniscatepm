_# Quarterly Business Review

**A template for the Quarterly Business Review (QBR) presentation — covering performance against goals, strategic insights, budget review, and recommendations for the next quarter.**

---

## Table of Contents

---

### Executive Summary

*Provide a high-level overview of the key findings and recommendations from the review. This section should be concise and impactful, summarizing the main takeaways for busy executives.*

### Performance Against Goals

*Detail the performance of the marketing campaigns and initiatives against the goals set for the quarter. Use charts, graphs, and data to illustrate the results. Focus on key metrics and KPIs.*

### Strategic Insights

*Analyze the data and provide strategic insights into what worked, what didn't, and why. Discuss key trends, challenges, and opportunities. This section should demonstrate a deep understanding of the business and the market.*

### Budget Review

*Review the budget for the quarter, including a breakdown of spending by channel and campaign. Discuss the ROI of the marketing investments and identify any areas of overspending or underspending.*

### Recommendations for Next Quarter

*Based on the findings of the review, provide clear and actionable recommendations for the next quarter. These recommendations should be specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, and time-bound (SMART).*

### Appendix

*Include any additional information, data, or supporting materials in the appendix. This could include detailed campaign reports, raw data, or market research findings.*_
