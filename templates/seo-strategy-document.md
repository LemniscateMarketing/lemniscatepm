# SEO Strategy Document

A template for documenting a complete SEO strategy, covering technical SEO, on-page optimisation, content strategy, link building, local SEO, and keyword targeting.

## Table of Contents

- [Executive Summary](#executive-summary)
- [Technical SEO](#technical-seo)
- [On-Page Optimisation](#on-page-optimisation)
- [Content Strategy](#content-strategy)
- [Link Building](#link-building)
- [Local SEO](#local-seo)
- [Keyword Targeting](#keyword-targeting)
- [Measurement & KPIs](#measurement--kpis)

## Executive Summary

*(Provide a high-level overview of the SEO strategy. Summarise the key objectives, major initiatives, and expected outcomes. This section should be clear, concise, and aimed at stakeholders who may not read the entire document.)*

## Technical SEO

*(Detail the technical aspects of the website that need to be addressed to improve search engine crawling and indexing. Cover topics such as site speed, mobile-friendliness, XML sitemaps, robots.txt, structured data, and crawl errors.)*

## On-Page Optimisation

*(Outline the strategy for optimising individual web pages. This includes the use of title tags, meta descriptions, header tags, image alt text, and internal linking. Specify the approach for optimising existing pages and the guidelines for new pages.)*

## Content Strategy

*(Describe the plan for creating and promoting content to attract and engage the target audience. Detail the types of content to be created (e.g., blog posts, articles, videos), the target keywords for each piece of content, and the content calendar.)*

## Link Building

*(Explain the strategy for acquiring high-quality backlinks to improve the website's authority and search rankings. Describe the outreach methods, the types of websites to target, and the metrics for evaluating potential link opportunities.)*

## Local SEO

*(If applicable, outline the strategy for improving visibility in local search results. This should include optimising the Google My Business profile, building local citations, and managing online reviews.)*

## Keyword Targeting

*(Present the keyword research and the target keywords for the SEO campaign. Group keywords by theme or user intent, and map them to specific pages on the website. Include primary and secondary keywords, as well as long-tail keywords.)*

## Measurement & KPIs

*(Define the key performance indicators (KPIs) that will be used to measure the success of the SEO strategy. This should include metrics such as organic traffic, keyword rankings, conversion rate, and return on investment (ROI). Describe the tools and methods for tracking and reporting on these KPIs.)*
