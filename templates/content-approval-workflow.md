## Approval Process Steps

*Outline the step-by-step process from content creation to final approval.*

1. **Draft Creation** — Agency creates content based on the approved strategy and content calendar.
2. **Internal Review** — Agency conducts internal quality check before submitting to client.
3. **Client Submission** — Content is shared with the client via the agreed platform.
4. **Client Review** — Client reviews content and provides feedback or approval within the agreed turnaround time.
5. **Revisions (if needed)** — Agency incorporates feedback and resubmits for review.
6. **Final Approval** — Client provides written approval (email, comment, or platform sign-off).
7. **Scheduling/Publishing** — Agency schedules or publishes the approved content.

## Escalation Path

*Define what happens when approvals are delayed, feedback is unclear, or disagreements arise.*

1. **Gentle Reminder** — Agency Account Manager sends a reminder after [X] business days of no response.
2. **Escalation to Secondary Approver** — If primary approver is unresponsive after [X] business days, the secondary approver is contacted.
3. **Schedule Adjustment** — If no approval is received within [X] business days, the content is deprioritised and the publishing schedule is adjusted.
4. **Formal Discussion** — Persistent delays are raised in the next scheduled client meeting for resolution.
