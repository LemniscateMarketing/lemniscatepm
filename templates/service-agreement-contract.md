# Service Agreement Contract

**Category:** Pre-sale

## Description

This document serves as a formal service agreement and contract between the digital marketing agency and the client. It outlines the scope of work, key deliverables, project timelines, payment terms, and other legal provisions to ensure a clear and mutually beneficial partnership.
