# Strategy Refresh Document

**Description:** A template for documenting strategic pivots or refreshes to the marketing plan based on quarterly learnings, market changes, or shifting client priorities.

## Executive Summary
*Provide a high-level overview of the proposed strategy refresh. Summarize the key findings from the performance review and market analysis, the core strategic changes being recommended, and the expected impact on business goals.*

## Performance Review
*Analyze the performance of the current marketing strategy over the last quarter. Use data and metrics to highlight successes, challenges, and areas for improvement. Include key learnings and insights that inform the need for a strategic refresh.*

## Market & Competitive Analysis
*Assess the current market landscape and competitive environment. Identify any new market trends, shifts in consumer behavior, or changes in the competitive landscape that necessitate a change in strategy. Include a SWOT analysis if applicable.*

## Strategic Recommendations
*Based on the performance review and market analysis, outline the specific strategic pivots or refreshes being recommended. Clearly explain the rationale behind each recommendation and how it addresses the identified challenges and opportunities.*

## Revised Marketing Plan
*Detail the updated marketing plan, including changes to target audience, messaging, channel strategy, and content plan. Explain how the revised plan aligns with the new strategic direction.*

## Success Metrics & KPIs
*Define the key performance indicators (KPIs) and success metrics that will be used to measure the effectiveness of the refreshed strategy. Ensure these metrics are specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, and time-bound (SMART).*

## Budget & Resource Allocation
*Outline any changes to the budget and resource allocation required to implement the refreshed strategy. Provide a clear breakdown of how resources will be reallocated to support the new plan.*

## Action Plan & Timeline
*Provide a detailed action plan and timeline for implementing the strategy refresh. Assign responsibilities for each action item and set clear deadlines to ensure timely execution.*
