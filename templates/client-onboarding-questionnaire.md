# Client Onboarding Questionnaire

**Category:** Onboarding

This comprehensive questionnaire is designed to gather all necessary information from a new client at the start of an engagement. It covers business goals, target audience, brand voice, competitors, existing assets, and access credentials to ensure a smooth and thorough onboarding process.
