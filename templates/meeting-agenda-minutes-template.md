> # Meeting Agenda & Minutes
> 
> **Category:** Monthly Operations
> 
> A template for structuring client meeting agendas and recording minutes, action items, and decisions.
> 
> ## Table of Contents
> 
> - **Agenda:** Outline of topics to be discussed.
> - **Attendees:** List of participants.
> - **Minutes:** Detailed notes from the meeting.
> - **Action Items:** Tasks assigned during the meeting.
> - **Decisions:** Key decisions made.
> 
> ---
> 
> ### Agenda
> 
> *Briefly outline the topics and talking points for the meeting. Include estimated times for each topic to keep the discussion on track.*

> -   **Topic 2:** [Description] (15 minutes)
> -   **Topic 3:** [Description] (5 minutes)
