# Social Media Strategy Document

A template for a comprehensive social media strategy — covering platform selection, content pillars, posting cadence, community management, influencer partnerships, and KPIs.

## Executive Summary

*Provide a high-level overview of the social media strategy. Summarize the key objectives, target audience, and expected outcomes.*     

## Target Audience

*Describe the primary and secondary target audiences. Include demographic, psychographic, and behavioral information.* 

## Platform Selection

*Detail the selected social media platforms and the rationale for choosing each. Explain how each platform aligns with the target audience and business goals.*

## Content Pillars

*Define the main themes and topics for content creation. Outline the types of content to be produced for each pillar (e.g., educational, entertaining, promotional).* 

## Posting Cadence

*Specify the frequency and timing of posts for each social media platform. Create a content calendar or schedule to guide implementation.*

## Community Management

*Outline the approach to engaging with the online community. Define the tone of voice, response time guidelines, and strategies for handling comments, messages, and mentions.*

## Influencer Partnerships

*Describe the strategy for collaborating with influencers. Detail the criteria for selecting influencers, outreach process, and campaign objectives.*

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

*List the metrics that will be used to measure the success of the social media strategy. Include specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, and time-bound (SMART) goals for each KPI.*
