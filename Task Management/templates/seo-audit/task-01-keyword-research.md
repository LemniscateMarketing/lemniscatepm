## Context

Keyword research is the foundation of this SEO audit. All downstream tasks
(on-page audit, content gap analysis, backlink audit) depend on this output.

**Client:** PLACEHOLDER_CLIENT
**Website:** PLACEHOLDER_DOMAIN

The goal is to map the full keyword universe: what they currently rank for,
what they should rank for, what their competitors rank for, and where the
highest-leverage opportunities are.

## Checklist

- [ ] Export current keyword rankings from Google Search Console (last 90 days)
- [ ] Run a seed keyword list based on client's products/services/audience
- [ ] Expand seed list using keyword research tool (Ahrefs, Semrush, or Google KP)
- [ ] Map keywords to search intent: informational / transactional / navigational
- [ ] Tag keywords by funnel stage: awareness / consideration / decision
- [ ] Identify quick wins: keywords ranking 5–20 with decent volume
- [ ] Identify high-priority gaps: high volume terms not ranking at all
- [ ] Group keywords into topic clusters
- [ ] Document primary, secondary, and supporting keyword for each key page
- [ ] Export final keyword map as CSV and upload to attachments

- Quick wins list contains at least 5 keywords ranking 5–20
- Topic clusters are clearly defined with cluster head terms
