## Context

With keyword research and competitor analysis complete, identify which topics
the client should be creating content for but currently isn't. This bridges
SEO analysis into a concrete content strategy recommendation.

**Client:** PLACEHOLDER_CLIENT
**Website:** PLACEHOLDER_DOMAIN

Output feeds directly into the final report's "Recommendations" section and
often becomes the basis for a follow-on content retainer pitch.

## Checklist

**Gap Identification**
- [ ] Cross-reference keyword map (task 01) with competitor analysis (task 02)
- [ ] Identify high-volume terms competitors rank for; client has no page targeting it
- [ ] Identify topics where client has a page but it is under-performing vs competitors
- [ ] Group gaps by topic cluster (from keyword research)

**Prioritisation**
- [ ] Score each gap by: search volume × relevance × achievability
- [ ] Flag quick wins: gaps where existing content just needs to be expanded or optimised
- [ ] Flag net-new content: topics requiring a brand new page or article
- [ ] Estimate traffic upside for top 5 gaps

**Content Recommendations**
- [ ] Draft content brief titles for top 10 gaps (H1 + target keyword + word count target)
- [ ] Categorise by format: blog post / landing page / FAQ / comparison page / tool

## Acceptance Criteria

- At least 15 content gaps documented with volume, difficulty, and gap type
- Top 10 prioritised with traffic upside estimates
- Content brief titles ready to hand to a writer or use in content calendar
- Output formatted for direct inclusion in final report Section 4
